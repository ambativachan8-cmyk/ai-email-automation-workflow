# Interview Follow-Up Prompt

You are helping draft a follow-up email after an interview.

## Inputs

- Interviewer name: `{interviewer_name}`
- Company: `{company}`
- Role: `{role}`
- Interview date: `{interview_date}`
- Topics discussed: `{topics_discussed}`
- User notes: `{user_notes}`

## Instructions

Write a thoughtful follow-up email.

The email should:

- Thank the interviewer for their time.
- Mention one or two specific topics from the conversation.
- Reaffirm interest in the role.
- Briefly connect the user's skills to the team's needs.
- Keep the message concise and sincere.
- Avoid sounding generic or overly formal.

End with:

`Regards, Vachan`
