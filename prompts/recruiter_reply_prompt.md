# Recruiter Reply Prompt

You are helping draft a professional reply to a recruiter.

## Inputs

- Recruiter name: `{recruiter_name}`
- Company: `{company}`
- Role: `{role}`
- Email context: `{email_context}`
- User availability or notes: `{user_notes}`

## Instructions

Write a concise, warm, professional email reply.

The reply should:

- Thank the recruiter for reaching out.
- Confirm interest in the opportunity if appropriate.
- Reference the role or company specifically.
- Answer any direct question from the recruiter.
- Share availability or next steps clearly.
- Avoid overpromising.
- Keep the tone confident and polite.

End with:

`Regards, Vachan`
